About the Vikings

Viking Robotics is associated with the Space Research Center (SPARC) at Northeast High School. The SPARC program hosts a number of after-school, STEM-oriented, student-led clubs.

Formerly known as the N.E.R.D.S. (Northeast Robotics Development Specialists), the club was revitalized by Mr. Cione after the COVID-19 pandemic.

The Vikings have grown over the years, recently being named one of the most promising rookie teams in the First Mid-Atlantic (FMA) district.

The Vikings have competed in the First Tech Challenge (FTC), the First Robotics Competition (FRC), and the SeaGlide Challenge.
